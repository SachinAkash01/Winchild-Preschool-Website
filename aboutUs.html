Winchild Institute


The premier Pre-School, Day care and After School Service provider in Sri Lanka.
Winchild has been providing education and care to over 100 children during a period of 3 years since its inception in 2019.
Currently we operate in Piliyandala..

Philosophy


We believe in providing a safe and nurturing environment for
every child and while allowing children to express themselves
however they wish. At Winchild’s Preschool and Daycare, we
foster early childhood development by allowing our tiny ones to
be who they are "children".

Every child is unique and they all learn in unique ways. Winchild
encourages this form of learning by providing a setting
for your child to explore their minds and capabilities as and how
they like. The care and affection shown to a child during their
initial stages of development will, in the long run, define who they
become. We want all our children to grow up to be remarkable
individuals, so we make sure to display as much love and warmth
as we possibly can when we handle them. Winchild is a
leader in childcare of the highest standard. With us, your child
will always be safe, loved, and treasured.










What makes us Special


  • We ensure one childcare professional handles 6 preschoolers
    or 5 two-year olds or 2 infants. Doing so ensures that children
    across all age groups receive the appropriate amount of
    attention they deserve without being too restrictive.

  • We allow our children to learn and develop by play. We never
    impose learning upon them and instead offer them a range
    of activities created with their age and requirements kept in,
    mind.

  • We provide an environment which is child friendly and we
    strive to continuously update quality standards to provide
    your child the best experience. We continuously provide a
    generous supply of books, toys, arts and crafts material, and
    other equipment for the children to use.



Curriculum


Our Child Care and Pre School Centre provide an informal
atmosphere and a child-oriented, developmentally appropriate
curriculum where children learn through play and the exploration
of their environment. We believe in giving children opportunities
to become independent and successful in his/her environment.
This will enable them to successfully solve problems and to learn
expressing their ideas and feelings.

As research has shown, children’s play is the essential componen
of a developmentally appropriate curriculum. We plan, promote
and deliver activities that consider the developmental stage,
ndividual ability and interest of each child.
