Play Group

(Age: 2 to 3 years)


We at Winchild Institute plan High-Quality Hands on
Play Programs in an appropriate manner to provide
challenging activities which are not too hard or too
easy—something toddlers can do with a little help. We
further choose materials, activities, and strategies in a
more meaningful manner;

What most toddlers need and what they are interested
in —like moving and being active throughout the day
with a lot of hands on, trial and error and imaginary
play activities.

What individual toddlers need—Planning activities on an
individual basis.. if they show interest in painting,
to provide paint, paper and brushes to develop their
aesthetic skill.












Lower Nursery

(Age: 3 to 4 years)


The Older Toddlers at Bankhill Educare follows a set curriculum
which covers all Four Developmental Domains;

  •     - Physical Development
  •     - Cognitives Development
  •     - Language Development
  •     - Social and Emotional Development


We all gather for the Morning Circle Time, where the children
get the ability to develop on their language skills, imagination,
self-esteem and self -confidence while participating in various
activities such as rhyme time, stories, puppet play, show and tell.



Upper Nursery

(Age: 4 to 6 years)


Our Preschool is set up in different sections :
science, literacy, writing, reading, dramatic play,
blocks and building, and nature corner. We believe that
children gain more knowledge when they work in small
groups, therefore we maintain a teacher -child ratio
of (1:10).

As research has shown, children’s play is the essential
componen of a developmentally appropriate curriculum.
We plan, promote and deliver activities that consider the
developmental stage, individual ability and interest of
each child.

















After School Care

(Age: 6 to 10 years)


We take in kids between ages of 6 years to 10 years.
The program focuses on both the care aspect and the
education aspect is monitored and aided by qualified
teachers.
